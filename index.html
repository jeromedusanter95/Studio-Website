// My approach

How I work

I build Android apps the way I build my own products: pragmatic, scalable, and business-driven.

Available for missions
01
Business first

I prioritize creating value for your business and users. My technical choices are always driven by your business goals and your time & budget constraints.

02
Pragmatic tech

I adapt architecture and technologies to the real needs of the project. A POC doesn't need the same complexity as a production app: I favor proportionate, efficient solutions over theoretical perfectionism.
